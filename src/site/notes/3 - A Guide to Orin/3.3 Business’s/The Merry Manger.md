An old run down inn on the edge of town within the walls of [[3 - A Guide to Orin/3.2 Cities and Landmarks/Brewhaven\|Brewhaven]]

Gronk spent a a deal of time here as he worked with the [[3 - A Guide to Orin/3.4 People/Blackthorn Vanguard\|Blackthorn Vanguard]]

Mr. and Mrs. Teagram run the inn. He runs the arena out back and she runs the front end/bar

The arena is a 1v1 arena where people from this low income part of [[3 - A Guide to Orin/3.2 Cities and Landmarks/Brewhaven\|Brewhaven]] come to drink and gamble their fortunes away.
